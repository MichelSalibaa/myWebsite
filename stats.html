Key Headlines

Regional and national internet metrics are constantly changing. The following figures summarise recent authoritative estimates (see references for full citations):

  • Global internet adoption reached roughly two‑thirds of the world’s population in 2024 according to major ICT reports.
  • Lebanon’s share of individuals using the internet was reported at about 83.5% in 2023 (World Bank collection).
  • Reported average broadband speeds in Lebanon during 2024 were relatively low compared with global leaders (single‑digit to low double‑digit Mbps ranges in several reports).
